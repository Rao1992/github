Actor politician
Nandamuri Balakrishna is an Indian film actor and politician known for his works predominantly in the Telugu cinema.
[2][3] He is the sixth son of Telugu film actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh 
N. T. Rama Rao. He entered the film industry as a child artist at the age of fourteen with the film Tatamma Kala (1974).
[4][5] Known for his dancing skills in a film career spanning forty years, he has acted in more than hundred feature films in a variety of roles.[6]
